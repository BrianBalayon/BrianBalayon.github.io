Some Information About Me


My name is Brian Anthony Mendoza Balayon, but you can just call me "Brian". Just a random fact, my mother told me if I was born a day later my name would've been "Valentino" because my birthday is February Thirteenth.

I am pursuing a Bachelor's degree in computer science at the University at Buffalo, graduating in the Spring of 2020. I'm currently a part of the Filipino American Student Association and the Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers. I graduated from Townsend Harris High School in 2016 with a Townsend Harris Honors Certificate and Advanced Regents Honors Diploma. I was a part of the FRC Team: 2601 Steel Hawks, Varsity Wrestling, Varsity Badminton, and Festival of Nations.

I am a New York City native, born in Brooklyn, and raised in Staten Island then Queens. When I'm in town, you can find me either taking the MTA or walking at a speed of two street blocks per minute. Usually I'll ride the 7 train into Manhattan and find a cafe to do some work, but on the occasional off chance, I'll be taking a stroll, often ending up two villages over in a random direction

I'm interested in cryptography, software design, graphic design, and poetry.


I just want to dedicate this little subsection to my wonderful support network. To my "Kuya" Franz, my "Kuya" Emil, my "Cambal" Ron, thank you for your guidance through college so far! To my friends, thank you for the good times we shared together and for the many more to come! To last but not least, my parents, Ana Flor M. Balayon and Silvio Herrera Balayon, are the Suns to my Earth. I wouldn't have been able to be where I am now without their unconditional love and support. Gusto ko lang masabi, Nanay, Tatay, mahal ko kayo lagi.
